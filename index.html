ABOUT
JOURNY

We planned to go to Kerala for a trip as well as Bharath's cousin's wedding. I remembered the journey was started by Koper.how we catch the train to go to Kerala(JUGAD).when we reached Bharat's native place his family was there. they welcome us very well. On the second day, we attend a wedding. I attended a first-time south Indian wedding. i ate the first time in banana leave and talking about food it was fabulous. after a wedding we started to travel to Kerala. we explore many places. after Kerala we went to Kanyakumari. in Kanyakumari we swam in an ocean. i never saw that kind of ocean.it was blueish and very cleaned.and after a Kanyakumari we went to TamilNadu.in TamilNadu, we saw a DR.Abdul kalam house and explored some places. after a TamilNadu, we suddenly planned to go to Ooty.and seriously we did lots of fun. we explored so many places. I still remember the 31st night. what happened with us and how much fun we did. and after exploring we came back to Mumbai.
